Mac and Cheese GPDS Demon List (v2.0.0)

Information about the Mac and Cheese Demon List

Official Discord

The Demon List is a collection of the hardest levels in the Mac and Cheese GPDS that adhere to certain standards, and are ranked by their difficulty. The Demon list accepts records, which are then placed on the list itself, if the footage meets the following requirements:
  • The records are achieved without hacks (FPS bypass is allowed, up to 360fps)
  • The record is achieved on the level that is listed on the site (refer to the level ID before submitting a record)
  • The record has either source audio or clicks/taps in the video. (Only for Hard Demons and over)
  • If possible, the recording has a previous attempt and the entire death animation is shown before the completion, except if the completion is on the first attempt.
  • The record does not use secret routes, bug routes and easy modes.
